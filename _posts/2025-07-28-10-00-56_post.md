The traffic in Guinea was crazy, even by West African standards. The loads were stacked absurdly high despite remnants of previous accidents littering the highways. Leaving Guinea we took one of the best roads, which unfortunately only encouraged the overloaded cars and trucks to drive even faster.

BoBs coming to understand that he's got it good as beasts of burden go.

Not so burdened after all,
