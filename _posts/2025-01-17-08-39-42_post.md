Hi BoBers, 

In September and early October I spent three weeks in Morvan, France visiting my friend Alice. It was a chance to relax and prepare myself and BoB for the trip ahead. Some high/low-lights

We built some furniture and I learnt how to render a wall as well as about home insulation and eco friendly construction (Damien did a PhD on it).

I did some work on BoB which taught me how little I know about bike maintenance. I got there in the end after weeks of frustration and prayers to Sheldon Brown.

I tapered off antidepressants which left me feeling miserable and kind of anxious, despite the wonderful setting. 

I learnt how to gather mushrooms. By the end of my time there I was bringing back half a kilo of mushrooms every day.

All in all a relaxing and productive time.

J&BoB
